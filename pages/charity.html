Light Up The World

Almost one billion people do not have access to electricity. Light Up The World brings solar electricity to families living off-the-grid.

About

Light Up The World (LUTW) is a Candadian non-profit organization focused on the principle that access to energy changes lives.
They work in communities that don't have access to electricity, and provide them with solar photovoltaic systems which help them transition away from using fuel-based lighting as well as helping them save money from using a lot of their income on batteries and charging devices.

LUTW has two offices - A head office located in Calgary, Canada, and a field office serving projects in South America based on Lima, Peru.
